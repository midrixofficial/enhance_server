Delete Your Account & Data

Last updated: July 1, 2026

You can request deletion of your Clarityix account and associated personal data at any time. This page explains how to do so, and what happens once your request is submitted.

How to request deletion

1

In the app

Open Clarityix → Settings → Account → Delete Account, and confirm the request.

2

By email

If you can't access the app, email support@clarityix.app from the address linked to your account with the subject line "Delete My Account," and we will verify and process your request.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile, including your name and email address on file.
  • Any photos, enhancement history, or preferences synced to your account.
  • Usage data linked to your account identifier.

What is retained

We may retain limited information, such as transaction records for in-app purchases, where required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes. This information is kept only as long as necessary and is not used for any other purpose.

Timeline

Account and data deletion requests are typically completed within 30 days of verification. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Questions

For any questions about this process, contact us at support@clarityix.app. See also our Privacy Policy.
