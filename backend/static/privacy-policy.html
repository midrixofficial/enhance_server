Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Clarityix ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Clarityix mobile application (the "App"), an AI-powered photo enhancement tool. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By downloading or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Photos and media. With your permission, the App accesses photos you choose from your device's gallery, or images you capture with your camera, so you can enhance them. Photos you select are processed to apply enhancement, and are not accessed without your explicit action.

Device and usage information. We may automatically collect device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, and general usage statistics (such as which features are used) to help us maintain and improve the App.

Account information (if applicable). If the App offers optional sign-in, we may collect your name, email address, and profile identifier from the sign-in provider (e.g. Google Sign-In) to sync your preferences across devices.

2. How We Use Information

  • To perform the photo enhancement, sharpening, restoration, and related image-processing features you request.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App's performance and stability.
  • To diagnose technical issues and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support requests, or changes to our policies.

3. Photo Processing & Third-Party AI Services

Some enhancement features may use secure, industry-standard cloud processing or third-party AI image-processing services to generate results. Where this occurs, only the specific photo you submit for enhancement is transmitted, solely for the purpose of generating your enhanced image, and is not used to train third-party models unless you are separately notified and asked to opt in. Processed images are deleted from processing servers shortly after your enhanced result is returned to your device.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. Enhanced photos are, by default, saved locally to your device. Where account sync is enabled, encrypted data may be stored with reputable cloud infrastructure providers. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers who help us operate the App (such as cloud hosting or crash-analytics providers), each bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations, or when required to comply with applicable law.

6. Your Choices & Rights

You can revoke camera or photo library permissions at any time through your device settings. Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

To request deletion of your account or associated data, please see our Account & Data Deletion page, or contact us at support@clarityix.app.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above, and, where appropriate, additional notice will be provided within the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@clarityix.app.
